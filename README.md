# The App Superhero 🦸‍♂️

Imagine you have a huge box of toys (your information), and a window where your friends (users) can ask for some toys. 

Normally, friends come and politely say, "Can I have one red car?" and you give it to them.

But sometimes, a sneaky friend (a bad guy) comes and says:
- "GIVE ME ALL THE TOYS RIGHT NOW!!" (Asking for too much)
- "I WANT THE TOY INSIDE THE BOX INSIDE THE BOX INSIDE THE BOX..." (Making things too complicated)

Our project is a superhero guard who stands by the toy window! 

Here is how our superhero works:
1. **The Smart Brain (Python Service):** This is a super smart robot brain that learns what a "polite request" looks like. It studies how bad guys ask for things and figures out if a friend is being sneaky.
2. **The Window Guard (JavaScript Middleware):** This guard stands at the toy window. Before they give out any toys, the guard asks the Smart Brain, "Does this friend sound sneaky?" If the Brain says "YES, SNEAKY!", the Guard stops them and says, "NO TOYS FOR YOU!"

It keeps your toy box safe by only letting the polite friends get toys. Your toys are safe! 🚀
